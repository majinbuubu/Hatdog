The Boy Who Cried Wolf is a fable about a shepherd boy who falsely alarms villagers by claiming a wolf is attacking his sheep. When a real wolf finally appears, the villagers ignore his cries, and the wolf attacks the flock.

The Boy Who Cried Wolf

by Aesop

Blueberries for Sal is about a little girl named Sal and her mother picking blueberries in the woods. Sal accidentally encounters a bear cub, and the two mistakenly swap places with their mothers.

Blueberries for Sal

by Robert McCloskey

The Milkmaid and Her Pail is a fable about a young milkmaid who daydreams of what she will do with the money she earns from selling her milk. As she imagines, she becomes so distracted that she spills her pail, learning a lesson about not counting on things before they happen.

The Milkmaid and Her Pail

by Aesop

The "Clever Fox" story is about a hungry crow who finds a piece of bread and plans to enjoy it. A cunning fox flatters the crow and tricks him into singing, causing the crow to drop the bread. The fox quickly grabs the bread, leaving the crow to regret his foolishness.

The Clever Fox

by Arun George
